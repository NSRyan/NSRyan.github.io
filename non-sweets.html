Garlic Loaf

Ingredients

  1. 1 3/4 c ~ Hot Water
  2. 1/2 tsp ~ Yeast
  3. 3 c ~ Flour
  4. Salt & Pepper
  5. Minced Garlic
  6. Rosemary
  7. Thyme
  8. Poppy Seeds
  9. Sesame Seeds
  10. Sugar
  11. Oil

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in the water with a little sugar
  2. Mix in the flour, and add a little oil
  3. Knead, then rest in an oiled bowl (covered) for around 14 hours
  4. Punch it down, and let it rest again for another 6 hours
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 F
  6. Knead in the garlic and spices
  7. Bake covered for 35-40 minutes, then bake uncovered until it's done

Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  1. 5-6 ~ Potatoes
  2. 3 tbsp ~ White Wine
  3. 2 tbsp ~ Butter
  4. 1/4 c ~ Heavy Cream
  5. 2 tbsp ~ Flour
  6. 3 tbsp ~ Chicken Stock
  7. Minced Garlic
  8. Salt & Pepper
  9. Green Onions
  10. Nutmeg
  11. Oregano

Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes into more or less even surface area
  2. Put into cold water with salt and bring to a boil
  3. In a seperate pot, make a roux with fragrant garlic, flour, and butter
  4. Add in wine and cream, then reduce

Braided Pesto Bread

Ingredients

  1. 3 c ~ Flour
  2. 2 1/4 tsp ~ Yeast
  3. 2 tsp ~ Sugar
  4. 1 1/4 c ~ Warm Water
  5. 1/4 c ~ Oil
  6. 1 tbsp ~ White Vinegar
  7. Pesto
  8. Salt

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in water and sugar
  2. Seperate the water into halves and add vinegar and flour to one half
  3. Add in salt and then alternate oil with the rest of the water
  4. Let rest for 2 hours, covered
  5. Treat it like a cinnamon roll with pesto
  6. Slice the mega roll lengthwise, press it down, and cut the halves again
  7. Braid the resulting strips and brush it in oil
  8. Let it proof again, then bake for 5 minutes at 400 F and finish (2 minutes) at 350 F

Baguette

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 tbsp ~ Yeast
  2. 2 tbsp ~ Honey
  3. 3 1/2 c ~ Flour
  4. 1 1/2 c ~ Warm Water
  5. Oil
  6. Salt
  7. Cornmeal

Instructions

  1. Make a Poolish the night before
  2. Bloom the yeast and honey with 1/2 c of water
  3. Incorporate flour, then mix in the rest of the water and salt
  4. Knead until smooth, then cover and rest in an oiled bowl for 25 minutes
  5. Divide into two loaves, and fold like an envelope until you have the right shape
  6. Coat the bottom in cornmeal, then place on a baking sheet
  7. Score the top and let it rest (covered)
  8. Preheat to 450 F with a second rimmed sheet under the rack you're going to use
  9. Bake till done (~15 minutes) with ice on the second sheet to steam

Sour Cream & Chive Dinner Rolls

Ingredients

  1. 5 1/3 c ~ Bread Flour
  2. 1/2 c ~ Milk
  3. 1 1/2 tsp ~ Yeast
  4. 1 c ~ Sour Cream
  5. 1/4 c ~ Sugar
  6. 2 1/4 tsp ~ Salt
  7. 3 ~ Eggs
  8. 1 stick ~ Butter
  9. 1/2 c ~ Chives (chopped)
  10. Flakey Salt
  11. Pepper

Instructions

  1. Make a tangzhong with milk, 1/3 c flour, 1/2 c water (stirring constantly)
  2. Remove from heat, then (seperatley) warm 2 tbsp of milk and bloom the yeast
  3. Mix the tangzhong with 2 eggs, 4 tbsp butter, 5 c flour, and the yeast mixture
  4. Take a shaggy dough and knead until tacky (10 min)
  5. Rest in an oiled bowl for 1 - 1 1/2 hours covered
  6. Butter a 9x13, and punch the dough
  7. Form balls, cover and rest in the dish for 45 minutes
  8. Preheat to 375 F, and use the last egg to wash the balls
  9. Salt and pepper, then bake for 25 - 30 minutes

Broccoli Cheddar Soup

Ingredients

  1. 1 ~ Small Onion
  2. 1/4 c ~ Butter
  3. 1/4 c ~ Flour
  4. 2 c ~ Half-and-half
  5. 3 c ~ Chicken Broth
  6. 2 ~ Bay Leaves
  7. 1 ~ Head Broccoli
  8. 1 ~ Peeled Carrot
  9. 2 1/2 c ~ Cheddar
  10. Nutmeg
  11. Salt & Pepper
  12. Cayanne
  13. Oil
  14. Minced Garlic

Instructions

  1. Chop the onion and dice the carrot
  2. Chop the broocoli and shred the cheese
  3. Soften the onion and carrot with the garlic and oil
  4. Make a roux, then add the cream to simmer
  5. Add the spices, then incorporate the broth
  6. Thicken, then add in the veggies
  7. Simmer, covered, until the veggies are soft
  8. Blend and add in the cheese with a little extra water if needed
