Vodka Cream

Description

Ingredients

  1. 8 oz ~ Crushed Tomatoes
  2. 1/2 c ~ Vodka
  3. 1 tbsp ~ Minced Garlic
  4. 2 tbsp ~ Butter
  5. 1/2 c ~ Heavy Cream
  6. 1/4 c ~ Parmesan
  7. 1 tbsp ~ Granulated Sugar
  8. Red Pepper Flakes
  9. Cayenne
  10. Oregano
  11. Oil
  12. Salt
  13. Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter, then add garlic and spices with a splash of oil
  2. Add the vodka and reduce about half
  3. Add in tomatoes, and simmer (covered) until it feels right
  4. Stir in the cheese and cream

Garlic Loaf

Description

Ingredients

  1. 1 3/4 c ~ Hot Water
  2. 1/2 tsp ~ Yeast
  3. 3 c ~ Flour
  4. Salt & Pepper
  5. Minced Garlic
  6. Rosemary
  7. Thyme
  8. Poppy Seeds
  9. Sesame Seeds
  10. Sugar
  11. Oil

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in the water with a little sugar
  2. Mix in the flour, and add a little oil
  3. Knead, then rest in an oiled bowl (covered) for around 14 hours
  4. Punch it down, and let it rest again for another 6 hours
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 F
  6. Knead in the garlic and spices
  7. Bake covered for 35-40 minutes, then bake uncovered until it's done

Peach Fennel Salad

Description

Ingredients

  1. 1 bulb (with stem and fronds) ~ Fennel
  2. 3 tbsp ~ Oil
  3. 2 tsp ~ Vinegar
  4. Salt & Pepper
  5. 5 ~ Peaches
  6. 2 tbsp ~ Honey
  7. Red Pepper Flakes
  8. Chipolte
  9. Dill

Instructions

  1. Cut out the fennel heart, then very thinly julienne the bulb
  2. Chop the stalks, and mince the fronds
  3. Soak the stalk and bulb in the vinegar for around 10 minutes
  4. Dice the peaches
  5. Mix everything together
  • Add heavy cream, cheese, peppers, green beans, and top with butter and bread crumbs for a casserole with the leftovers

French Gnocchi

Description

Ingredients

  1. 1 lbs ~ Ricotta
  2. 1/4 c ~ Parmesan
  3. 1 3/4 c ~ Flour
  4. 1 tbsp ~ Oil
  5. 2 ~ Eggs
  6. 1 ~ Egg Yolk
  7. Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine the eggs, cheese, and oil
  2. Add in salt & pepper with whatever spices you're feeling like
  3. Slowly incorporate flour until the mixture is smooth and tacky, but not sticky
  4. Shape into logs and flour as needed
  5. Boil until they float in salted water
  6. Finish in sauce

Italian Gnocchi

Description

Ingredients

  1. Eggs
  2. Flour
  3. Potatoes
  4. Salt & Pepper
  5. Oil
  6. Butter
  7. Garlic
  8. Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Half or quarter the potatoes, and put in cold water
  2. Salt the water, and bring to a boil
  3. Shred, then combine the rest of the ingredients to form a dough
  4. Knead, then rest for 20 minutes in the fridge
  5. Roll & cut into pillows
  6. Boil in salted water until they float
  7. Pan fry in oil, butter, some garlic, and rosemary

Salmon Wellington

Description

Ingredients

  1. Puff Pastry
  2. Salmon Fillets
  3. Mustard
  4. Spinach
  5. Sliced Mushrooms
  6. White Wine
  7. Minced Garlic
  8. Salt & Pepper
  9. Eggs
  10. Butter
  11. Dill
  12. Lemon
  13. Oil

Instructions

  1. Sautee mushrooms in butter, oil, garlic, and seasonings
  2. Add a splash of wine and the spinach
  3. Season the salmon, and give it a quick sear on both sides
  4. Preheat according to your puff pastry recipe
  5. Layout a couple sheets of pastry, and center a fillet on it
  6. Rub mustard on the fillet, and cover with the mushroom-spinach concoction
  7. Seal with an egg wash
  8. Poke some holes in the top, and bake according to the puff pastry (until golden)

Mac 'n Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  1. Pasta
  2. Velveta
  3. Cheddar
  4. Parmesan
  5. Mozzerella
  6. Butter
  7. White Wine
  8. Mustard
  9. Chicken Stock
  10. Heavy Cream
  11. Bread Crumbs
  12. Salt & Pepper
  13. Cayanne
  14. Paprika
  15. Minced Garlic
  16. Chives

Instructions

  1. Brown garlic in butter, melt in the velveta and other cheeses
  2. Add chicken stock, wine, and season
  3. Cook the pasta, and preheat the over to 350 F
  4. While the pasta cooks, to the cheese sauce add in the cream and mustard
  5. Finish the pasta in the sauce, the pour into a baking dish
  6. Bake with bread crumbs and butter on top till golden
