Vodka Cream

Ingredients

  1. 8 oz ~ Crushed Tomatoes
  2. 1/2 c ~ Vodka
  3. 1 tbsp ~ Minced Garlic
  4. 2 tbsp ~ Butter
  5. 1/2 c ~ Heavy Cream
  6. 1/4 c ~ Parmesan
  7. 1 tbsp ~ Granulated Sugar
  8. Red Pepper Flakes
  9. Cayenne
  10. Oregano
  11. Oil
  12. Salt
  13. Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Melt the butter, then add garlic and spices with a splash of oil
  2. Add the vodka and reduce about half
  3. Add in tomatoes, and simmer (covered) until it feels right
  4. Stir in the cheese and cream

Garlic Loaf

Ingredients

  1. 1 3/4 c ~ Hot Water
  2. 1/2 tsp ~ Yeast
  3. 3 c ~ Flour
  4. Salt & Pepper
  5. Minced Garlic
  6. Rosemary
  7. Thyme
  8. Poppy Seeds
  9. Sesame Seeds
  10. Sugar
  11. Oil

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in the water with a little sugar
  2. Mix in the flour, and add a little oil
  3. Knead, then rest in an oiled bowl (covered) for around 14 hours
  4. Punch it down, and let it rest again for another 6 hours
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 F
  6. Knead in the garlic and spices
  7. Bake covered for 35-40 minutes, then bake uncovered until it's done

Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  1. 5-6 ~ Potatoes
  2. 3 tbsp ~ White Wine
  3. 2 tbsp ~ Butter
  4. 1/4 c ~ Heavy Cream
  5. 2 tbsp ~ Flour
  6. 3 tbsp ~ Chicken Stock
  7. Minced Garlic
  8. Salt & Pepper
  9. Green Onions
  10. Nutmeg
  11. Oregano

Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes into more or less even surface area
  2. Put into cold water with salt and bring to a boil
  3. In a seperate pot, make a roux with fragrant garlic, flour, and butter
  4. Add in wine and cream, then reduce
  5. Add salt, pepper, chicken stock, and green onions
  6. Heat unitl the mixture rises then remove from heat and add nutmeg
  7. Strain potatoes when they are fork tender
  8. Mash the potatoes, gradually mashing in the cream mixture until you like the consistency

French Gnocchi

Ingredients

  1. 1 lbs ~ Ricotta
  2. 1/4 c ~ Parmesan
  3. 1 3/4 c ~ Flour
  4. 1 tbsp ~ Oil
  5. 2 ~ Eggs
  6. 1 ~ Egg Yolk
  7. Salt & Pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine the eggs, cheese, and oil
  2. Add in salt & pepper with whatever spices you're feeling like
  3. Slowly incorporate flour until the mixture is smooth and tacky, but not sticky

Italian Gnocchi

Ingredients

  1. Eggs
  2. Flour
  3. Potatoes
  4. Salt & Pepper
  5. Oil
  6. Butter
  7. Garlic
  8. Rosemary

Instructions

  1. Half or quarter the potatoes, and put in cold water
  2. Salt the water, and bring to a boil
  3. Shred, then combine the rest of the ingredients to form a dough
  4. Knead, then rest for 20 minutes in the fridge
  5. Roll & cut into pillows
  6. Boil in salted water until they float
  7. Pan fry in oil, butter, some garlic, and rosemary

Salmon Wellington

Ingredients

  1. Puff Pastry
  2. Salmon Fillets
  3. Mustard
  4. Spinach
  5. Sliced Mushrooms
  6. White Wine
  7. Minced Garlic
  8. Salt & Pepper
  9. Eggs
  10. Butter
  11. Dill
  12. Lemon
  13. Oil

Instructions

  1. Sautee mushrooms in butter, oil, garlic, and seasonings
  2. Add a splash of wine and the spinach
  3. Season the salmon, and give it a quick sear on both sides
  4. Preheat according to your puff pastry recipe
  5. Layout a couple sheets of pastry, and center a fillet on it
  6. Rub mustard on the fillet, and cover with the mushroom-spinach concoction
  7. Seal with an egg wash
  8. Poke some holes in the top, and bake according to the puff pastry (until golden)

Stuffed Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  1. 6 ~ Chicken Breasts
  2. 1 bag ~ Spinach
  3. 1 c ~ Mushrooms
  4. 8 oz ~ Ricotta
  5. 1 c ~ Parmesan
  6. 3 tbsp ~ White Wine
  7. 2 tbsp ~ Butter
  8. Minced Garlic
  9. Salt & Pepper
  10. Dill
  11. Lemon Juice
  12. Paprika
  13. Twine

Instructions

  1. Chop the mushrooms, dill, and spinach
  2. Sautee the mushrooms with butter and wine until they start to soften
  3. Add the spinach, salt & pepper, and dill
  4. Let cool and preheat the oven to 375 F
  5. Butterfly the chicken (alternatively, cut pockets and you probably won't need twine)
  6. Mix the rest of the ingredients with the mushrooms
  7. Stuff the chicken, and tie it up with twine
  8. Season the breasts
  9. Brown the outsides on medium high heat in a pan (this is not necessairly to cook everything)
  10. Put everything in the oven until it's cooked (around 15 minutes)
