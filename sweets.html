Orange Oil Cake

Ingredients

  1. 5 ~ Eggs
  2. 3 c ~ Granulated Sugar
  3. 1 1/2 c ~ Orange Juice
  4. 3 1/2 c ~ Flour
  5. 2 tsp ~ Salt
  6. 1 1/2 c ~ Oil (fruity if possible)
  7. 1 1/2 tsp ~ Baking Powder
  8. Orange Zest

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F
  2. Grease and flour a 12 cup bundt pan
  3. Whip the eggs and sugar until the mixture starts to lighten and cream
  4. Add salt, zest, and powder
  5. Alternate oil and flour (ending with flour)
  6. Fold in the juice
  7. Bake for 1 1/4 hours

Banana Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

  1. 1 ~ Banana (ripe)
  2. 3 tbsp ~ Granulated Sugar
  3. 1 tsp ~ Vanilla
  4. 1 c ~ Dark Chocolate
  5. 1 c ~ Heavy Cream
  6. 2 ~ Egg Whites

Instructions

  1. Put the bowl and whisk into the freezer for ~10 min before you start
  2. Mash the banana and melt the chocolate
  3. Whip the egg whites until they foam
  4. Separately, whip the cream until stiff adding in the sugar, salt, and vanilla as you go
  5. Fold eveything together and refridgerate for 2 hours

Oatmeal Cookies

Ingredients

  1. 2 1/2 c ~ Flour
  2. 3 1/2 c ~ Rolled Oates
  3. 1/2 tsp ~ Baking Soda
  4. 1/2 tsp ~ Baking Powder
  5. 1 c ~ Butter
  6. 1 c ~ Brown Sugar
  7. 1/2 c ~ Granulated Sugar
  8. 2 ~ Eggs
  9. 1 c ~ Raisins
  10. 1 c ~ Walnuts
  11. 1 1/2 tsp ~ Vanilla
  12. Cinnamon, nutmeg, salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat to 350 F

Banana Bread/Muffins

Ingredients

  1. 3 ~ Bananas
  2. 2 tbsp ~ Honey
  3. 1 1/2 tsp ~ Baking Soda
  4. 1 stick ~ Butter
  5. 3/4 c ~ Brown Sugar
  6. 1 tsp ~ Vanilla
  7. 2 ~ Eggs
  8. 2 c ~ Flour
  9. Cinnamon, salt, nutmeg

Instructions

  1. Preheat to 350 F
  2. Whisk together both sugars, softened butter, eggs until they lighten
  3. Mash the bananas and mix them in
  4. Incorporate vanilla and honey
  5. Add salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg
  6. Grease the pan, and bake for ~50 minutes
  • To turn into muffins, add a splash of milk, more baking soda, 1/2 c flour, an extra banana, 1/4 c coco powder
  • Bake at 450 F for 2 minutes and then lower to 350 F for 5 minutes

Peanut Butter Kisses

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 c ~ Flour
  2. 1 tsp ~ Baking Soda
  3. Salt
  4. 1 stick ~ Butter
  5. 1/2 c ~ Sugar
  6. 1/2 c ~ Brown Sugar
  7. 1/2 c ~ Peanut Butter
  8. 1 ~ Egg
  9. 1 tsp ~ Vanilla
  10. 48 ~ Hershey Kisses

Instructions

  1. Cream butter and sugars
  2. Add egg and fluff until it lightens
  3. Add peanut butter, vanilla, baking soda, salt
  4. Mix in flour
  5. cover and refridgerate until set (~1 hr)
  6. Preheat to 350 F
  7. Roll into balls, make sure they stay cold
  8. Roll in sugar
  9. Bake for 10 minutes
  10. Immediatley, press the kisses into the cookies after you pull them out

Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

Dough
  1. 5 ~ Eggs (4 yolks, 1 whole)
  2. 1/4 c ~ Sugar
  3. 6 tbsp ~ Butter (melted)
  4. 3/4 c ~ Buttermilk
  5. 4 c ~ Flour
  6. 2 1/4 tsp ~ Yeast
  7. Salt
  8. Oil
Filling
  1. 1 c ~ Brown Sugar
  2. 1 1/2 tbsp ~ Butter
  3. Cinnamon
  4. Cumin
  5. Nutmeg
  6. Sumac
Icing
  1. 1/4 c ~ Cream Cheese
  2. 3 tbsp ~ Milk
  3. 1 1/2 c ~ Powdered Sugar

Instructions

  1. Mix the wets for the dough
  2. Add 1/2 the flour and yeast
  3. Alternate the rest of the flour and sugar
  4. Knead until tacky (8 min)
  5. Let double in a warm place for ~ 2 hours in an oiled bowl
  6. Roll out the dough into the sheet and coat in the filling
  7. Roll it up and cut it, and set them in a covered buttered dish to rest overnight in the fridge
  8. Preheat to 350 F, and boil some water
  9. Bake for 30 minutes
  10. Mix the icing

Biengets

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/4 c ~ Warm Milk
  2. 1 1/2 tsp ~ Dry Active Yeast
  3. 1/3 c ~ Sugar
  4. 3 1/2 c ~ Flour
  5. 1 ~ Egg
  6. 2 tbsp ~ Melted Butter
  7. Powdered Sugar
  8. Honey

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in the milk with sigar
  2. Mix in 1 1/2 c of the flour with butter and the egg
  3. Slowly add the rest of the flour until the dourgh forms
  4. Knead or hook the dough for around 6 minutes
  5. Rest for 34-40 minutes, then punch down
  6. Heat oil to 370 F
  7. Roll into rectangles and cut into pillows
  8. Fry until done, then coat in sugar and honey to serve immediatley

Peanut Butter Cheesecake

Ingredients

Cheesecake:
  1. 3 packages (room temp) ~ Cream Cheese
  2. 1/2 c ~ Sugar
  3. 1 tsp ~ Vanilla
  4. 1 1/4 c ~ Peanut Butter
  5. 1/4 c ~ Sour Cream
  6. 1 1/4 c ~ Heavy Cream
  7. 3/4 c ~ POwdered Sugar
  8. Salt
Cookie Base:
  1. 1 ~ Egg
  2. 1 c ~ Brown Sugar
  3. 1 c ~ Peanut Butter
Brownie Base:
  1. 2/3 c ~ Flour
  2. 1/2 tsp ~ Baking Powder
  3. 1/2 tsp ~ Vanilla
  4. 1 stick ~ Butter
  5. 1 c ~ Brown Sugar
  6. 2 ~ Eggs
  7. 2/3 c ~ Chocolate

Instructions

Cheese Cake:
  1. Whip heavy cream and powdered sugar to peaks
  2. Add vanilla and salt
  3. Separately, mix the sugars, cream cheese, and sour cream with peanut butter
  4. Fold the two together
Cookie:
  1. Preheat to 350 F
  2. Grease a 9" round cake pan (springform if you can)
  3. Cream egg and sugar
  4. Mix in peanut butter
  5. Bake for 16 min without filling
  6. After it has cooled, add filling and refrigerate for ~2.5 hr
Brownie:
  1. Preheat to 350 F
  2. Grease a 9" round cake pan (springform if you can)
  3. Cream butter, eggs, and sugar
  4. Add salt, vanilla, baking powder, and melted chocolate
  5. Add in flour
  6. Bake for 25-30 min without filling
  7. After it has cooled, add filling and refrigerate for ~2.5 hr

Peach Roulade

Ingredients

Cake
  1. 4 ~ Eggs
  2. 2/3 c ~ Sugar
  3. 1 tsp ~ Syrup
  4. 3/4 c ~ Flour
  5. Salt
Whipped Cream
  1. 2 c ~ Heavy Whipping Cream
  2. 1/2 c ~ Brown Sugar
Filling
  1. 2 tbsp ~ Butter
  2. 2 tbsp ~ Sugar
  3. Cinnamon
  4. Nutmeg
  5. 16 oz ~ Peaches

Instructions

  1. Preheat to 375 F, and line a 15x10x1 with parchment
  2. Whip eggs and sugar until it's light
  3. Fold in the rest of the cake ingredients
  4. Bake for 10 minutes
  5. Flip the cake out onto another sheet of parchment, and remove the o.g. parchment and replace it with a clean one
  6. Roll the cake and chill it while you prepare the rest of the dish
  7. Freeze the mixer bowl, then whip the cream and add in sugar
  8. In a pot, melt the butter with everything else
  9. Cook down the peaches until the sauce is nappe
  10. You got the rest

Grasshopper Ice Cream Tart

Ingredients

Torte
  1. 1 pkg ~ Oreos
  2. 1 pkg ~ Andes Menthe Thins
  3. 3 tbsp ~ Melted Butter
  4. 3 tbsp ~ Coco Powder
  5. 2 tbsp ~ Powdered Milk
Filling
  1. 1 quart ~ Heavy Cream
  2. 21 oz ~ Sweetened Condensed Milk
  3. 2 tbsp ~ Mint Extract
  4. Green Food Dye
  5. Mini Chocolate Chips
Ganache
  1. 1/2 c ~ Heavy Cream
  2. 1 tbsp ~ Light Corn Syrup
  3. 1/2 c ~ Chocolate
  4. Salt

Instructions

Torte
  1. Pulse 2/3 of the oreos with butter, milk powder, and coco until it feels like wet sand
  2. Put into a springform and freeze for 25 minutes
Filling
  1. Whip everything until it's good.
  2. Freeze until the crust is ready, then fold in crumbled thins and oreos
  3. Freeze for at least 10 hours
Ganache
  1. Simmer cream, and mix in chocolate, syrup, and salt
  2. Let sit for around 5 minutes until chocoalte has melted
  3. Stir, then pour over the cake
  4. Freeze until the ganache is firm to the touch

Ginger Pumpkin Cheesecake

Ingredients

Crust
  1. 1 1/2 c ~ Ginger Snap Cookies
  2. 1/4 c ~ Butter
  3. 2 tbsp ~ Brown Sugar
  4. Salt
Filling
  1. 24 oz ~ Cream Cheese
  2. 15 oz ~ Pumpkin Puree
  3. 3 ~ Eggs
  4. 1/4 c ~ Sour Cream
  5. 1 1/2 c ~ Sugar
  6. 1 tsp ~ Vanilla
  7. Cinnamon
  8. Nutmeg

Instructions

Crust
  1. Process cookies, butter, salt, and sugar
  2. Press into a springform and freeze for 30 minutes
Filling
  1. Soften cream cheese, and beat in puree, sugar, eggs, sour cream, cinnamon, vanilla, and nutmeg
  2. Bake like on a baking sheet with ice for 50 minutes (should be a very loose shake)
  3. Turk off oven and crack the door for around 15 minutes (should be a little loose shake)
  4. Cool for 30 minutes, then refridgerate for 4 hours

Butterscotch Rice Krispies

White Chocolate

  2. Coconut Flakes
  3. Coconut Oil

Butterscotch
  1. White Chocolate
  2. Sugar

  4. Vanilla
