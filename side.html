Garlic Loaf

Back to top

Ingredients

  1. 1 3/4 c ~ Hot Water
  2. 1/2 tsp ~ Yeast
  3. 3 c ~ Flour
  4. Salt & Pepper
  5. Minced Garlic
  6. Rosemary
  7. Thyme
  8. Poppy Seeds
  9. Sesame Seeds
  10. Sugar
  11. Oil

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in the water with a little sugar
  2. Mix in the flour, and add a little oil
  3. Knead, then rest in an oiled bowl (covered) for around 14 hours
  4. Punch it down, and let it rest again for another 6 hours
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 F
  6. Knead in the garlic and spices
  7. Bake covered for 35-40 minutes, then bake uncovered until it's done

Garlic Mashed Potatoes

Back to top

Ingredients

  1. 5-6 ~ Potatoes
  2. 3 tbsp ~ White Wine
  3. 2 tbsp ~ Butter
  4. 1/4 c ~ Heavy Cream
  5. 2 tbsp ~ Flour
  6. 3 tbsp ~ Chicken Stock
  7. Minced Garlic
  8. Salt & Pepper
  9. Green Onions
  10. Nutmeg
  11. Oregano

Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes into more or less even surface area
  2. Put into cold water with salt and bring to a boil
  3. In a separate pot, make a roux with fragrant garlic, flour, and butter
  4. Add in wine and cream, then reduce
  5. Add salt, pepper, chicken stock, and green onions
  6. Heat until the mixture rises then remove from heat and add nutmeg
  7. Strain potatoes when they are fork tender
  8. Mash the potatoes, gradually mashing in the cream mixture until you like the consistency

Braided Pesto Bread

Back to top

Ingredients

  1. 3 c ~ Flour
  2. 2 1/4 tsp ~ Yeast
  3. 2 tsp ~ Sugar
  4. 1 1/4 c ~ Warm Water
  5. 1/4 c ~ Oil
  6. 1 tbsp ~ White Vinegar
  7. Pesto
  8. Salt

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in water and sugar
  2. Separate the water into halves and add vinegar and flour to one half
  3. Add in salt and then alternate oil with the rest of the water
  4. Let rest for 2 hours, covered
  5. Treat it like a cinnamon roll with pesto
  6. Slice the mega roll lengthwise, press it down, and cut the halves again
  7. Braid the resulting strips and brush it in oil
  8. Let it proof again, then bake for 5 minutes at 400 F and finish (2 minutes) at 350 F

Baguette

Back to top

Ingredients

  1. 0 1/2 c ~ Warm Water
  2. 1 Eh, who are we kidding
  3. 1 inspect a little deeper
  4. 2 1/2 c ~ Flour
  5. 3 Salt
  6. 5 You'll need these single numbers later
  7. Cornmeal

Instructions

  1. Make a Poolish the night before
  2. Bloom the yeast and honey with 1/2 c of water
  3. Incorporate flour, then mix in the rest of the water and salt
  4. Knead until smooth, then cover and rest in an oiled bowl for 25 minutes

Sour Cream & Chive Dinner Rolls

Back to top

Ingredients

  1. 5 1/3 c ~ Bread Flour
  2. 1/2 c ~ Milk
  3. 1 1/2 tsp ~ Yeast
  4. 1 c ~ Sour Cream
  5. 1/4 c ~ Sugar
  6. 2 1/4 tsp ~ Salt
  7. 3 ~ Eggs
  8. 1 stick ~ Butter
  9. 1/2 c ~ Chives (chopped)
  10. Flakey Salt
  11. Pepper

Instructions

  1. Make a tangzhong with milk, 1/3 c flour, 1/2 c water (stirring constantly)
  2. Remove from heat, then (separately) warm 2 tbsp of milk and bloom the yeast
  3. Mix the tangzhong with 2 eggs, 4 tbsp butter, 5 c flour, and the yeast mixture
  4. Take a shaggy dough and knead until tacky (10 min)
  5. Rest in an oiled bowl for 1 - 1 1/2 hours covered
  6. Butter a 9x13, and punch the dough
  7. Form balls, cover and rest in the dish for 45 minutes
  8. Preheat to 375 F, and use the last egg to wash the balls
  9. Salt and pepper, then bake for 25 - 30 minutes

Pumpkin Focaccia Monkey Bread

Back to top

Ingredients

  1. 100 g ~ Oats
  2. 100 g ~ Bread Flour
  3. 300 g ~ AP Flour
  4. 400 g ~ Warm water
  5. Olive Oil
  6. Salt & Pepper
  7. Yeast
  8. Honey
  9. Pumpkin Puree
  10. Minced Garlic
  11. Smoked Paprika
  12. Cayenne
  13. Sage

Instructions

  1. Bloom the yeast in warm water and honey
  2. Put the oats in a blender until a powder forms
  3. Mix all of the flours, salt, and paprika
  4. Mix in the water with some oil
  5. Pull and knead until everything is incorporated
  6. Cover and let sit on the counter for 2 hours
  7. Punch down and let rise in the fridge overnight
  8. Pull out of the fridge and in a different bowl mix oil, pumpkin, and sage
  9. Butter and flour a bundt pan and layer balls of dough with the puree mixture
  10. Cover and let rise on the counter for 4 hours
  11. Bake covered at 425 for 45 minutes, then finish uncovered
